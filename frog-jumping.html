Frog Jumping Puzzle

Number of frogs:

Rules of the game:

  • The goal is to get all the frogs to the same lily pad.
  • All frogs on a lily pad will jump together.
  • The frogs on a lily pad must jump left or right, by the same number of lily pads as there are frogs.
  • Queen frogs must be on the top of a pile.
  • Lazy frogs must always be on the bottom of a pile.
  • Waterlogged lilypads cannot be used.

How to play:

  • Click on a lily pad to select it.
  • Clicking on a second lily pad will make all the frogs jump to the second lily pad.
  • To make a queen, click on the crown, followed by a lily pad.
  • To make a lazy frog, click on the brown frog, followed by a lily pad.
  • To make a waterlogged lilypad, click on the crossed out frog, followed by a lily pad.
  • To start a new game, slide the frog slider.
